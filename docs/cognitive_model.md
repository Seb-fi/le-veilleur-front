\# Cognitive Model

\#\# Core principle

The system does not optimize information quantity.

It optimizes:  
\- understanding  
\- prioritization  
\- cognitive load reduction  
\- signal emergence  
\- contextual relevance

\---

\#\# Non-goals

The system must NOT optimize:  
\- click maximization  
\- doomscrolling  
\- engagement loops  
\- outrage  
\- addictive behavior

\---

\#\# Desired effects

Users should feel:  
\- better informed  
\- less cognitively overloaded  
\- exposed to meaningful novelty  
\- capable of building mental models

\---

\#\# Exploration vs exploitation

The system must balance:  
\- familiar relevant topics  
\- emerging weak signals  
\- controlled serendipity

\---

\#\# Contradiction policy

The system should avoid:  
\- ideological bubbles  
\- excessive confirmation bias  
\- repetitive reinforcement

Contradictory perspectives are valuable.

\---

\#\# Audio philosophy

Audio is:  
\- editorial  
\- curated  
\- concise  
\- cognitively efficient

Not audiobook-style narration.

\---

\#\# Success metrics

Success is NOT:  
\- session duration  
\- click count

Success IS:  
\- briefing completion  
\- perceived usefulness  
\- discovery quality  
\- cognitive clarity  
\- return frequency
